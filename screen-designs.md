## Design System Notes

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: #007AFF (iOS Blue)
- **Secondary**: #34C759 (iOS Green)
- **Warning**: #FF9500 (iOS Orange)
- **Error**: #FF3B30 (iOS Red)
- **Background**: #F2F2F7 (iOS Light Gray)
- **Text**: #000000 (iOS Black)

### Typography
- **Headers**: SF Pro Display, 28pt, Bold
- **Subheaders**: SF Pro Display, 22pt, Semibold
- **Body**: SF Pro Text, 17pt, Regular
- **Captions**: SF Pro Text, 13pt, Regular

### Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (8pt radius)
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows, rounded corners
- **Input Fields**: Clear borders, focus states
- **Icons**: SF Symbols for consistency

### Accessibility
- High contrast ratios
- Large touch targets (44pt minimum)
- VoiceOver support
- Dynamic Type support
- Color-blind friendly palette
